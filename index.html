About LTS

LTS was founded in July 2023. Our primary motivation for establishing this shop was driven by a deep passion for authentic Mexican cuisine and a desire to share our love for tacos with the community. At "Little Taco Shop," our mission is to serve delicious and satisfying tacos while creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for our customers. We strive to use only the finest and freshest ingredients, sourcing locally whenever possible, to ensure the highest quality in every bite. Our goal is to provide a memorable dining experience, where customers can enjoy a variety of flavorful tacos crafted with care and creativity. We aim to showcase the rich culinary heritage of Mexico and deliver exceptional customer service, leaving a lasting impression on each and every visitor.

Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appar in the United States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States in the early 20th century. The exact date is difficult to pinpoint, but it is believed that tacos gained popularity among Mexican immigrants and Mexican-American communities in the southwestern states, such as Texas and California, during the 1910s and 1920s. Over time, tacos gradually spread throughout the country and became a beloved staple of American cuisine. Today, tacos are widely enjoyed across the United States, with various regional and international influences shaping their diverse and delicious fillings and preparations.
